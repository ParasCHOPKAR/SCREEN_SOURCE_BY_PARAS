MacDermid Products

MacDermid products are renowned for their precision, durability, and high-performance qualities in the screen printing industry. Designed for industrial applications, MacDermid films, emulsions, and chemicals ensure consistent results, sharper prints, and enhanced productivity. Whether you are working on fine detail graphics or large format designs, MacDermid materials deliver unmatched reliability, making them the preferred choice for professional screen printers worldwide.

Gloss and Antiglare Films

Enable demanding industrial and consumer HMI applications with our flexible.

Autoflex films combine flexibility with durability, offering excellent chemical and abrasion resistance. Available in gloss and antiglare finishes, they are ideal for demanding industrial and consumer HMI applications.

Autotex® XE

Textured hardcoated films designed for extended life in outdoor conditions.

Autotex XE is a polyester film with specially designed hardcoat and primer layers. Unlike conventional films that can delaminate, become brittle, or flake under extreme conditions, the various layers in Autotex XE are formulated to resist ultraviolet light, moisture, and wide temperature variations.

Autostat™ AHU

Hazy heat-stabilized polyester film with optimized surface slip properties .

Autostat AHU is a high-quality, haze-resistant, heat-stabilized polyester film with excellent surface slip properties for superior sheet handling. It is available in sheets and rolls with tight dimensional and squareness tolerances.

Autostat™ CUS

heat-stabilized polyester film with a slip for excellent film handling characteristics.

Autostat CUS is a high-quality, clear, heat-stabilized polyester film with a slip treatment on both sides for excellent film handling characteristics. It is available in sheets and rolls, with tight dimensional and squareness tolerances, and excellent lay-flat properties for accurate print registration. Applications include flexible circuitry, membrane switch circuitry, flexible flat cables, sensors, and RFID antennas.

Autoflex® EB Gloss

Highly durable and flexible hardcoated polyester films with excellent optical properties.

Autoflex EB is a hard-coated polyester film range designed to provide high abrasion, chemical, and solvent resistance, along with excellent optical clarity and flexibility. These films can be embossed and support extended switch life. Available in gloss and antiglare finishes, Autoflex EB offers solvent and UV ink primer options for both screen and digital printing, including UV-curable inks.

Autostat™ CT

Clear, heat-stabilized polyester film with tight dimensional and squareness tolerances.

Autostat CT is a high-quality, clear, heat-stabilized polyester film, pre-primed on both sides, and available in sheets and rolls with tight dimensional and squareness tolerances. Applications include flexible circuitry, membrane switch circuitry, flexible flat cables, sensors, and RFID antennas.

Autostat™ WP20

Stabilized polyester film with low transparency for high contrast printing.

Autostat WP20 is a heat-stabilized, white opaque polyester film featuring a primer coating on one surface. The primed side has a higher gloss level than the untreated surface. With very low transparency and high brightness, it serves as a versatile print medium, offering excellent mechanical strength and good dimensional stability.

Autostat™ HT

Hazy, heat-stabilized polyester film, with an ink primer coating on both sides.

Autostat HT is a high-quality, hazy, heat-stabilized polyester film with an ink primer coating on both sides. It is available in sheets and rolls, with tight dimension and squareness tolerance, and excellent lay-flat properties for accurate print registration. Applications include flexible circuitry, membrane switch circuitry, flexible flat cables, sensors, and RFID antennas.

Autostat™ WT

Stabilized polyester film with an opaque white finish and double-sided adhesion treatment.

Autostat WT is a high-quality, opaque white, heat-stabilized polyester film with an ink primer coating on both sides. It is available in sheets and rolls, with tight dimensional and squareness tolerances and excellent lay-flat properties for accurate print registration. Applications include flexible circuitry, membrane switch circuitry, flexible flat cables, sensors, and RFID antennas.

Windotex™

Windotex produces clear and durable display windows when selectively printed on films.

Windotex is specially designed for Autotex hard-coated films to produce display windows in selected areas of the textured surface. For Autotex film, which has a textured first surface, screen or digital printing is used to print the required graphic design on the second or print surface of the sheet. It's also possible to screen-print with Windotex onto selected areas of the first surface to form crystal-clear windows for electronic displays. Windotex Gloss and Windotex Antiglare can be mixed in different proportions to achieve different effects. The resulting window is wear-resistant, scratch-resistant, and chemical-resistant.

XtraForm® 3D Gloss

A deep formable hardcoated high gloss finish polycarbonate film for automotive interiors.

XtraForm 3D is a formable polycarbonate film with a UV-curable hardcoat, designed for deep-draw 3D film insert molding (FIM) applications using the XtraForm process. The advanced coating technology delivers a consistently black appearance, even in deeply formed areas. XtraForm 3D enables smart surfaces that are feature-rich, visually striking, and highly durable.

XtraForm® AntiGlare

A deep formable polycarbonate film hardcoated with a consistent antiglare finish.

XtraForm Antiglare is a formable polycarbonate film with a UV-curable hardcoat. It is designed for deep-draw 3D film insert molding (FIM) applications using the XtraForm process. The antiglare coating disperses reflections, making displays clearer and safer in different lighting conditions. With XtraForm Antiglare, displays and smart surfaces can be feature-rich, visually stunning, and highly durable.

Autotex® PC Fine

A flexible hardcoated film with a smooth texture designed for laptop touchpads.

Autotex PC consists of a high-quality polycarbonate film coated with a highly durable and chemically resistant textured hardcoat. The texture does not contain fillers, promoting outstanding clarity and consistency from batch to batch. This product is used for applications where the rigidity and clarity of thick-gauge polycarbonate are required, along with an abrasion- and chemically resistant surface. The hardcoat is finished with a consistently smooth texture (Fine or Touch), which is ideal for laptops and other touchpads.

XtraForm® 2D Gloss

A polycarbonate film hardcoated with gloss and antiglare finishes.

XtraForm 2D is a hard-coated polycarbonate film designed to allow shallow forming while retaining its abrasion-resistant properties. This product has a chemically bonded, UV-cured hard surface coating in a gloss or antiglare finish.

Fototex™

A UV curable texturing lacquer for printing texture finishes over selected areas of Autoflex films.

Fototex is a UV-curable texturing lacquer designed for use with all Autoflex films or uncoated polycarbonate. This product offers a simple and cost-effective method for producing a range of durable surface textures. For example, when printed on automotive dials, it can be used to produce flat, matte, and non-reflective finishes.

XtraForm® Texturing Liquids

Formable and printable lacquers .

XtraForm Fine and XtraForm Supermatt are solvent-based, UV-curable, screen-printable texturing lacquers. They are designed to enable a range of matte textured finishes to be printed selectively onto XtraForm films using the XtraForm process. After drying, they can be formed to produce deep-draw 3D parts, which, when UV cured, will give a hard, textured finish. They can also be printed onto graphic inks for automotive dial applications.

Autotex® Softouch

A unique soft-to-touch hardcoated polyester film for HMI applications .

Autotex Softouch's distinctive, silky texture is incredibly tactile yet robust, and it still delivers exceptional resistance to a wide range of chemicals, solvents, surface wear, and abrasion.

Autotex® Fine, Velvet, & Touch

Polyester films with a flexible textured hardcoat.

The range of finishes provides a tactile feel for the user, while the hardcoat keeps the printed graphic bright and clear—even in high-touch applications.

Autotex® Antimicrobial (AM)

Flexible hardcoated films combining durability.

Autotex AM combines unique UV-cured hardcoat technology for durability and chemical resistance with antimicrobial technology that is active 24/7 to inhibit the growth of stain- and odor-causing bacteria on the surface. The result is a surface that is robust for cleaning and stays hygienically cleaner and fresher between routine cleans. This product is available in Fine and Touch textures. Fine is the texture typically used for membrane touch switches and equipment overlay applications. Touch is a finer-feel texture originally designed for computer touchpads.

Autotex® Steel

Flexible film with a consistent and durable textured hardcoat that presents the touch .

Autoflex PC offers a combination of outstanding clarity, low haze, and low yellowness levels, enabling high-level resistance to wear, scratches, and chemicals. This product is ideally suited for applications such as fascias and nameplates, where the rigidity and clarity of polycarbonate are required.

Autoflex® PC Gloss

Highly durable flexible hardcoated polycarbonate films with excellent optical clarity.

These films offer excellent dimensional stability, superior surface properties, and are ideal for high-precision screen printing. Common in overlay applications.

XtraForm® 3D Matt

A deep formable polycarbonate film hardcoated with a premium matt .

XtraForm 3D Matt, a low-gloss, formable hardcoated polycarbonate film, meets automotive designers' needs for a premium vehicle interior experience that is both soft to the touch and visually stunning. The smooth, softer touch of XtraForm 3D Matt enables accurate and confident smart surface interactions, enhancing fidelity for slide controls such as those used for HVAC and interior lighting.

Print & Peel

A water-based lacquer designed to protect specific areas of film during processing.

Print & Peel is a water-based, screen-printable lacquer designed for selective application on a wide range of surface finishes, serving as an easily removable protective mask. Print & Peel offers a cost-effective alternative to expensive, difficult-to-position over laminates. It protects surfaces from scratches and scuffs during production and shipment
