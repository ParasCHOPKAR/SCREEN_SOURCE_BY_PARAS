Nitto Industrial Tapes

Innovating Adhesion for a Better Tomorrow: From precision manufacturing to everyday applications, Nitto leads the way in high-performance tape solutions. With a legacy of Japanese innovation spanning over 100 years, Nitto’s adhesive technologies are trusted worldwide to deliver durability, safety, and efficiency in the most demanding environments.

Why Choose Nitto Tapes?

  • Unmatched Adhesion Strength: Engineered to bond securely to metals, plastics, glass, and composites—even under extreme conditions.
  • Temperature & Weather Resistance: Performs flawlessly from freezing cold to scorching heat, ensuring reliability year-round.
  • Specialized Applications: Solutions for automotive, electronics, construction, packaging, and more.
  • Clean Removal & Residue-Free: Designed for easy application and removal without damaging surfaces.
  • Eco-Friendly Options: Innovative low-VOC, recyclable, and sustainable tape products for a greener future.

Nitto tapes are more than just adhesives—they’re problem-solving tools that help industries stick to their goals.
