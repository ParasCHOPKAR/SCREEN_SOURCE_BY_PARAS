POLYESTER MACDERMID INDUSTRIAL FILMS

MacDermid Enthone Industrial Films is a range of high-specification coated plastic films and printable protective lacquers designed to improve performance and aesthetics in industrial applications.

Hard Coatings Enhance the Base Film by Imparting:

  • Scratch, abrasion, and chemical resistance while maintaining flexibility
  • Glare control with low haze
  • Excellent clarity and low color cast

Autotex Film Range

Autotex is a series of printable textured polyester (PET) and polycarbonate (PC) films designed for durable applications.

  • Highly durable with resistance to scratching, scuffing, chemicals, and solvents
  • Can be embossed for extended switch life
  • Supports selective printing with Windotex lacquer

Autotex (PET): Fine, Velvet, Touch, Steel, Softouch finishes

Autotex PC: Coated for touchpads

Autotex AM: Antimicrobial with Microban®

Autotex XE: Outdoor durable

Autoflex Film Range

Autoflex is a hard-coated polyester and polycarbonate film range used in advanced display and interface technologies.

  • Flat panel display units
  • Touch screens and mobile phone lenses
  • Smart cards and EL lamp displays
  • Membrane Touch Switches (MTS)

Autoflex EB: Flexible, scratch-resistant, high optical clarity

Autoflex PC: High scratch resistance, low haze

Autostat

Autostat is a heat-stabilized polyester film used in smart surface circuitry such as IME, MTS, keyboards, and keypads. Its heat stability allows curing of conductive inks at high temperatures with minimal distortion or lay-flat issues.

XTRAFORM™ 3D – Formable Hardcoated Polycarbonate Film

XtraForm 3D is engineered for deep draw FIM applications. Its improved coating maintains color consistency even in extreme forming areas.

  • XtraForm 3D 2L: Gloss both sides, protective laminate
  • XtraForm 3D M HCL: Gloss/matte combo, protective hardcoat
