ORAFOL Product Range

Premium Adhesive Tapes & Vinyl Films

ORAFOL offers a wide range of adhesive tapes and vinyl products known for their high quality and performance in various applications. Their adhesive tapes, including ORABOND®, ORAFLEX®, and ORATAPE®, are designed for permanent or reversible bonding on diverse surfaces, while their vinyl products, like ORACAL® and ORAJET®, are popular for signage, decoration, and other applications. These products are valued for durability, environmental resistance, and ease of application.

Adhesive Tapes:
• ORABOND® – Double-sided high-performance tapes with strong bonding, UV resistance, and durability for multiple surfaces.
• ORAFLEX® – Specialized cliché mounting tapes for printing applications.
• ORATAPE® – Application tapes like MT80P for vinyl graphics with residue-free removal and dimensional stability.

Vinyl Products:
• ORACAL® – Durable, vibrant vinyl films for signage, vehicle graphics, and decoration.
• ORAJET® – Printable vinyl films for high-quality inkjet graphics.

Key Features: High performance adhesion, long-lasting durability, user-friendly application, versatile use cases, and industry-leading quality.
